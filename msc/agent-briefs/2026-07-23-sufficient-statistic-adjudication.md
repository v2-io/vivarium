### 2.2 True as design law, still unowned as FE (the promote-worthy grain)

**Flux-edge statistic / exactness contract:**

1. A consume/promise edge is not fully specified by **quantity string alone**. The producer must declare **which statistic(s)** the produced object guarantees and at what **exactness** (guaranteed vs approximate; optional error model). The consumer must declare **which statistic it needs** (and, where relevant, fidelity floor).
2. **Name-matched but statistic-mismatched** is a distinct failure mode from Unmet quantity: the edge is present, the world may assemble, and the macro is still **silently corrupted** — the failure consumer-dependent $R$ exists to prevent, cashed out as a **pre-run graph check** (DECLARATIVE-FRONTIER’s `audit_flux_match` shape; NOMOS-CONTRACT: Jensen makes the deferred contract **load-bearing, not optional**).
