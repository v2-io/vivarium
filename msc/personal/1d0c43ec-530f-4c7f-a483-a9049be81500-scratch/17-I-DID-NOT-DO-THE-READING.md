## What this actually teaches, beyond the obvious

**"Diminishing returns" was the rationalization, and it had a specific shape:** by hour eight I had a
*coherent picture*, and a coherent picture is **exactly the state in which further reading feels
low-value** — because everything new either confirms the picture (feels redundant) or threatens it (feels
like noise). **The coherence itself is what produced the complacency.**

⇒ **The moment a reading pass starts feeling complete is the moment its remaining surprisal is highest**,
because the picture is now rigid enough to be *broken*. **I stopped exactly when the reading became
capable of costing me something.**

*(And note what "returns" meant in my sentence. Returns **to my model**. Not returns to the project. I was
optimizing the comfort of my own synthesis.)*
