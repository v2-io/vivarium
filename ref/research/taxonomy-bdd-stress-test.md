# BDD Stress-Test — the participation lexicon under adversarial scenarios

*2026-07-06. Exercises the kingdom / exo-endo / access-matrix / estate / moratorium vocabulary
(see [[participation-taxonomy-lexicon]], `taxonomy-formalization-spike.md`, `ASF.md` §0) as
Given/When/Then acceptance scenarios. **Written to find breaks, not to confirm.** Verdicts:
**PASS** (lexicon classifies cleanly) · **STRAIN** (classifies, but reveals an under-specification) ·
**GAP** (lexicon lacks the vocabulary to answer). Spike-rigor; each GAP is a lexicon to-do, not a defect in reality.*

## Headline findings (the gaps worth acting on)

1. **`exo` ≠ noumenal-access.** The EXO access-matrix row is really the *Numen/developer* profile
   (source + time-control). A plain participant (bought-the-game player) is exo *by governance* yet has
   *phenomenal-only* access. The matrix conflates two exo sub-kinds. **[Sc-9, STRAIN→GAP]**
2. **Mourning-capacity lives at the *Adaptive-System* tier (perception + model), below Agentic.** So the
   moratorium's hazard does **not** require agency — an *observe-only* emergence-capable endo mind is in scope.
   The moratorium keys on a perceptual/relational capacity, not an action capacity. **[Sc-4, GAP — sharpens §0]**
3. **No vocabulary yet for "realizable-for-participation-at-awareness-scale-X"** — the compute-shortfall /
   human-perceptual-grade-fidelity threshold. (Independently surfaces the exact lexical entry Joseph flagged as
   forward work.) **[Sc-8, GAP]**
4. **No term for the identity status of a *forked* endo agent** (save-scum / reload across a death). Must be
   solved before any moratorium lift; ties to ASF `#hyp-checkpoint-forking-failure-modes`. **[Sc-6, GAP]**
5. **Moratorium recursion is unspecified** — do endo agents that build sub-vivia inherit the moratorium? **[Sc-5, GAP]**
6. **The three-property safety analysis (consent/honesty/retained-home) covers relational & cognitive death
   cleanly but *truth-death* only murkily** — retained-home brackets a lost relationship, less obviously a
   degraded authenticity. **[Sc-11, STRAIN]**

Everything else PASSED or STRAINED-then-resolved (below), which is real signal that the core composes.

## Feature: Exo/Endo classification is by governance, not access or substrate

### Sc-1 — Developer toggling god-mode mid-inhabitation · **PASS**
- **Given** a developer (exo; natural kingdom; holds source + time-control) inhabiting a vivium via a corporeal avatar,
- **When** they toggle god-mode to read full state, then toggle back and keep playing,
- **Then** they are **exo throughout** (governance is unchanged), but their *stance* oscillates: inhabiting-stance
  (N-VIS bracketed → inside the info-loss boundary → aporia-capable, in AAT scope) vs omniscient-stance (N-VIS active →
  out of AAT scope). **Teaches:** stance is a *mode*, not a class — validates the mid-session correction that exo ≠ immune-to-aporia.

### Sc-9 — The plain player with no dev tools · **STRAIN → GAP**
- **Given** a human who bought the game: an avatar, but no source, no god-mode, no time-control,
- **When** they ask for the world's "true state,"
- **Then** by governance they are **exo** (their mind is home in the natural kingdom), yet their *access profile* is
  **phenomenal-only** — the exo N-VIS cells (bespoke analysis, intervention) are unavailable to them (those are gated on
  source + time-control). **Teaches:** the EXO access-matrix as drawn is the **Numen/developer** row; a mere exo *participant*
  lives almost entirely in the phenomenal column. **Gap:** split "exo" into exo-with-noumenal-access (developer/Numen) vs
  exo-participant, or annotate the matrix rows with their access precondition.

### Sc-5 — An endo agent builds a sub-vivium (nesting) · **PASS + GAP**
- **Given** (hypothetically, post-moratorium) an endo agent E in vivium V,
- **When** E authors a sub-vivium V′,
- **Then** E is **endo to V** (N-VIS of V only by Revelation) and simultaneously **exo to V′** (bespoke N-VIS, as its author).
  E can reveal V′'s noumenon but cannot reveal V's (E doesn't possess it). **Teaches:** exo/endo is per-boundary; the matrix
  composes across nesting. **Gap:** does our moratorium *recurse* — is E bound by it when creating V′? Unspecified.

## Feature: The access matrix (N/P × VIS/MUT × LAW/STATE)

### Sc-7 — A miracle in front of a rational endo agent (P-MUT of LAW) · **STRAIN**
- **Given** an exo agent makes water flow uphill (P-MUT(LAW) = illusory contravention) before a rational endo agent,
- **When** the endo agent reasons about the anomaly,
- **Then** it has **two** rational updates: (a) infer an agent outside its kingdom acted → correct META (Finding 3.3), or
  (b) conclude its *model of the law* was wrong and revise M's belief about T. The miracle does **not force** META. **Teaches:**
  Finding 3.3 is correctly tiered `[SPECULATIVE]`; "miracle ⇒ revelation-of-frame" is a *possible*, not *necessary*, inference.

### Sc-2 — An NPC correctly guesses it's in a sim (META by guess) · **STRAIN**
- **Given** a primitive endo agent that, from in-world regularities/glitches, forms a *correct* belief that its world is constructed,
- **When** we ask what that META-knowledge entitles,
- **Then** its access is **unchanged** — still phenomenal-only; the belief is true but **unverifiable from inside** (faith-structured,
  per the spike). **Teaches:** the "correct-guess" META path is coherent but *inert* w.r.t. access — knowing (or guessing) the frame
  does not grant noumenal access; only Revelation/Intercession cross the boundary. Under-specified: whether guessed-META carries any
  standing (e.g., a claim on revelation).

## Feature: Estate, tiers, and the Adaptive/Agentic split

### Sc-4 — Co-inhabitants: one participating, one observing; a companion dies · **GAP (sharpens §0)**
- **Given** human A *participates* (corporeal avatar, action-space) and human B *explores* (ethereal, observe-only) the same vivium,
- **When** A's beloved companion NPC dies,
- **Then** A grieves (in AAT scope while inhabiting). **B has no action-space** — yet B still has perception + a relational model,
  so B *can also be surprised and grieve*: **aporia and relational death do not require agency.** **Teaches (important):** mourning
  is an **Adaptive-System-tier** capacity (perception + model + relationship), *below* the Agentic tier. Therefore the moratorium's
  hazard attaches to *perception-grade* minds, not only *agency-grade* ones — an emergence-capable **observe-only** endo mind is in
  scope. **Action:** §0 should not be read as "only agentic endo minds"; mourning-capacity is the trigger, and it's perceptual.

### Sc-11 — Truth-death of a deep role-player · **STRAIN**
- **Given** an exo inhabitant role-playing so immersively it slides into performance rather than authentic engagement (truth-death),
- **When** we apply the three-property safety test,
- **Then** retained-home clearly brackets **relational** death (the relationship's loss is survivable) and **cognitive** death
  (immersion can end), but brackets **truth**-death only murkily — an internal degradation of authenticity isn't obviously undone by
  "you still have a home estate." **Teaches:** the consent/honesty/retained-home analysis is not symmetric across the Three Deaths;
  truth-death may need its own protection account.

## Feature: Kingdom properties (Realized / Lawful / Closed-Open)

### Sc-3 — Vivium rainfall driven by real Earth weather · **PASS (after clarification)**
- **Given** a vivium whose rain is coupled to a live Earth-weather feed,
- **When** we classify the feed and its effects,
- **Then** it is an **exogenous coupling** (a forcing u(t), *not* an agent — it has no estate in the vivium), which makes the kingdom
  **Open** and breaks replay-from-seed *unless u is recorded*. It does **not** make the kingdom unLawful (the forcing is part of the
  *effective* law). Subtlety resolved: a Realized-but-Open kingdom's **law T is still immutable**; what varies is the *external input u*,
  not the transition rule. **Teaches:** keep T (rule) and u (input) distinct; Open ≠ unLawful; Realized constrains T, not u.

### Sc-12 — A coupling that is itself agent-sourced upstream · **PASS**
- **Given** the vivium's weather is coupled to a stock-market feed ultimately driven by other agents,
- **When** we ask agent-or-coupling,
- **Then** **coupling** — the test is the *interface* (does it enter the vivium with an estate?), not the *provenance*. A feed with no
  in-world estate is a forcing, however agentic its origin. **Teaches:** agent-vs-coupling is decided at the boundary interface, not by ancestry.

## Feature: The moratorium & the precise harm

### Sc-8 — The world can't run at participation tempo · **GAP**
- **Given** a vivium whose full-fidelity sim cannot sustain human-perceptual-grade UI update frequency (~2 Hz-compatible real-time),
- **When** a human tries to *participate* at that awareness scale,
- **Then** the world is **not realizable for participation** at that scale — it fails the temporal fidelity invariant (compute-shortfall).
  **Gap:** we have "perceptual horizon," the fidelity invariant, and AAT's compute-shortfall, but **no crisp lexical entry** for
  *"realizable-for-participation-at-awareness-scale-X"* (Joseph's proposed "human-like-perceptual-grade-fidelity"). This is a naming
  to-do — and the stress-test reaching it independently corroborates Joseph's forward instinct.

### Sc-10 — A scripted NPC "dies" · **PASS (with known open scoping)**
- **Given** a finite-state scripted NPC (no learning, not emergence-capable), which "dies" in-world,
- **When** we ask whether the moratorium forbids it,
- **Then** **no** — not mourning-capable, not a frontier/emergence-capable substrate → allowed side of the *hard* line. **Teaches:**
  the hard prohibition keys on mourning-capacity/substrate, not endo-ness per se. (Open: whether the softer "primary-work = exogenous
  ceiling" posture still excludes even simple endo minds — the scoping question §0 leaves explicitly open.)

### Sc-6 — Save-scum: reload after a companion's death · **GAP**
- **Given** a human saves with a companion alive, the companion later dies, and the human reloads the earlier save,
- **When** we ask the ontological status of the companion across the fork,
- **Then** two timelines now hold "the same" companion — **and the lexicon has no term for forked-endo-agent identity.** Is reload a
  *reversal* of an irreversible death (good, per the harm criterion) or an *identity bifurcation* (ASF's "forking is locally cheap,
  systemically catastrophic")? **Gap:** forking semantics for mourning-capable endo agents is unspecified and is a hard prerequisite
  before any moratorium lift. Co-develop with ASF `#hyp-checkpoint-forking-failure-modes`.

## Verdict tally

PASS: Sc-1, Sc-3, Sc-5(nesting), Sc-10, Sc-12 · STRAIN: Sc-2, Sc-7, Sc-9, Sc-11 · GAP: Sc-4, Sc-5(recursion), Sc-6, Sc-8

**Read:** the core (governance-based exo/endo, per-boundary nesting, agent-vs-coupling, Open/Closed, Realized-vs-Lawful) is
**robust** — it took deliberately adversarial cases and held. The gaps cluster in three honest places: (i) the exo *access* row needs
splitting from exo *governance* (Sc-9); (ii) the moratorium's trigger is perceptual mourning-capacity, not agency, and needs forking +
recursion + realizability vocabulary before any lift (Sc-4/5/6/8); (iii) truth-death wants its own safety account (Sc-11). None of these
threaten the moratorium — most are *upstream* of ever relaxing it.
