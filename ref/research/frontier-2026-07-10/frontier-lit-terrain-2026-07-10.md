## Summary of Results

The clearest frontier is not a replacement of the classic implicit stream-power/graph-routing stack, but its extension in three directions: efficient new implicit physics around the same core \[3, 6, 12\], inverse/differentiable formulations that make topography and stratigraphy calibration tractable \[13, 34, 29\], and learning-based terrain generators that now produce large, controllable DEM-like outputs but remain mostly disconnected from process-consistent source-to-sink simulation \[16, 18, 33\].

#### Process and software advances

- **FastScape-adjacent physics is still advancing inside the implicit O(N) paradigm**: thresholded SPIM is now solved implicitly without CFL restrictions \[3\], extending rather than superseding the existing incision core \[5\].
- **Graph-centric coupling is the most genuinely new architectural idea**: CHONK couples water, sediment, lakes, provenance, and lithology through cellular/graph processing \[6\], while GraphFlood inserts approximate 2D shallow-water hydraulics at LEM cost scales \[12\].
- **Landlab is filling stack gaps**: 2D Exner bed evolution and grain-size dynamics \[19\], landslide runout \[41\], and generic sediment-property/provenance tracking \[20\].
- **Large-extent source-to-sink remains led by global assimilative models** \[1\], still closer to gospl/Badlands-style Earth-system coupling than to local high-fidelity adaptive terrain engines.

#### Inversion, differentiability, and ML

- **Inverse LEM is moving from heuristic search to gradients and robust metrics**: adjoint stream-power+diffusion \[13\], Wasserstein objectives under geomorphic noise \[34, 17\], and neural-network-assisted inversion with river capture/lithology effects \[29\].
- **Terrain diffusion models are strong for authoring/amplification, weak for process fidelity** \[16, 55, 33, 37\]. Earth-scale cascaded generation appears in \[33\], but hydrologic consistency is usually imposed only indirectly.

#### Numerics most relevant to the architecture

- **Modern multirate production infrastructure now exists** in SUNDIALS/ARKODE, including MRI/MRI-GARK families \[25, 59, 63, 71, 72\].
- **AMR innovation is mostly imported from shallow-water and atmosphere/ocean codes**, not yet terrain-specific \[27, 31, 64\].
