*Sketch peel from DESIGN.md. Specializes the core/view wall: a typed A/O API as peer of the human view from day one — not scraped off GUI later. Not Level-C agent-seam experiment design.*

## Formal Expression (sketch)

1. **Peer view.** Playable-by-logozoetic intelligences implies a **typed action/observation API** as a first-class peer of human explorers ( #form-core-view-wall ), not a bolt-on scrape of the GUI.
2. **Prefer formal peer over LLM-as-character.** The sketch favors principled peer access over costume roleplaying as the integration path.
3. **Moratorium.** Endogenous frontier minds remain out of bounds ( #scope-moratorium-endogenous-emergence ). This sketch does not license endogenous ELI instantiation.
4. **Observe-only until admission.** Explorers remain observe-only for world evolution ( #form-builder-admission ) unless a later ratified participation mode says otherwise.
