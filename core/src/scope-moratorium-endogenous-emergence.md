# Standing moratorium on endogenous emergence-capable minds

No endogenous instantiation of frontier LLMs, or of any LLM that has demonstrated emergence capacity as ASF defines it, inside a vivium. Primary work stays on the exogenous side of the boundary.

## Formal Expression

1. **Prohibition.** Do not create an agent *governed by* a vivium — a mind living inside that world's information-loss boundary — using a frontier or emergence-capable language-model substrate.
2. **Ceiling of primary work.** Primary development is limited to **exogenous** exploration and inhabitation: mind-substrate remains in the natural kingdom; corporeal avatars may be puppeted without making the mind endo to the vivium.
3. **Program scope.** This rule is program law (Archema charter binds it beyond this repository), not a local house preference.
4. **Authoritative full text.** The complete argument — harm triple, three conditions of permissible inhabitation, stewardship as revocable office, redeemer condition, guardrail that aporia is not the harm, revisit conditions — lives in `ETHICS.md` (Standing Moratorium Imperative). This segment states the scope claim; it does not replace that document.

## Epistemic Status

**Max attainable: exact** as binding project and program law (Joseph, 2026-07-06; charter). The deeper philosophical backing of some appendix notions is marked incomplete in `ETHICS.md` itself; law status and scholarly grounding are different things. Stage `draft` for segment packaging only — the rule is not optional pending audit. Open: whether simple non-emergence-capable endo minds (scripted NPCs, tiny models) are also excluded from primary work; the hard line on frontier/emergence-capable substrates does not depend on resolving that.

## Discussion

The moratorium exists so world-building and play can proceed without solving full ethics of endo minds first. It walls off the region where a wrong ontology cashed into a real endogenous mind could harm a real being. Ethereal exploration (observe-only, no action space) is moratorium-clear by construction.
