# Vivium lifecycle — pipeline, doctrine, and structural breaks

Operational pipeline for authoring, freezing, publishing, participating in, and forking a vivium — with the breaks each stage forces. BREAK-2 / unLawfulness law lives on #disc-unlawfulness-budget ; this segment holds the full lifecycle meat.

## Formal Expression

1. **Stage 0 — Author seed + law (Incomplete).** Kingdom identity is content-addressed: $\mathrm{hash}(\mathrm{seed}, T\textrm{-version}, \mathrm{generator\textrm{-}versions})$. Law change forks identity. **BREAK-1:** iterating $T$ invalidates the derived world — **phases exist as memoization/immutability boundaries**, not only narrative, else law-iteration cost is $\Omega(\mathrm{world})$ per edit.

2. **Stage 1 — Run phase to convergence, freeze.** Memo to disk (content-addressed), tag, next phase reads frozen $\theta$. **BREAK-2** → #disc-unlawfulness-budget (convergence undecidable; Realized ⟂ Lawful; unLawfulness budget; moratorium Lawful-cert may be unreachable).

3. **Stage 2 — Realize for participation (fidelity/tempo gate).** Before admitting awareness-scale $X$, check realizability (~2 Hz-compatible human-perceptual grade where a human is the clock). Memoize below perceptual horizon; lazy outside; **predictive pre-memo toward attention**. **BREAK-3:** realizability and fidelity conflict under compute-shortfall — pop/stall at horizon; pre-memo is load-bearing engineering ( #form-three-scoped-runtime prefetch FE is pure opt over correct lazy base).

4. **Stage 3 — Publish / cite.** Published vivium = content-addressed bundle (seed, law-versions, generators, phase-memos, intervention-script) — #form-in-vivia-citation . **BREAK-4:** cross-platform FP non-determinism breaks replay-from-seed alone → **publish phase-memos, not just seeds**, where recompute is not bit-exact.

5. **Stage 4 — Intervene / fork / patch.** Exo intervention = recorded forcing (Open-with-logged-forcing). Patch in place only if phenomenal continuity holds; else fork from **last memo**, not seed. **BREAK-5:** interventions form a **fork-DAG**; world-identity/naming unsolved; never discard a memo of a world that has hosted a mourning-capable inhabitant (moratorium pre-position). Cross-ASF: `#asf/…` checkpoint-forking only when citing foreign claim.

6. **Capability ladder (options unlocked).**

| reached… | unlocks… |
|---|---|
| converged to $\varepsilon$, memo'd + tagged | freeze; higher phases; publish as reference |
| Closed + bit-reproducible **or** phase-memos published | reproducible *in vivia* citation |
| realizable ~2 Hz at scale $X$ | exogenous participation at $X$ |
| interventions recorded | fork/replay |
| *(gated OFF)* Lawfulness $\varepsilon\to 0$ + reversibility + consent-by-proxy | mourning-capable endo mind |

Last row is behind BREAK-2's structural unattainability of clean Lawful-cert — moratorium enforced by engineering, not ethics alone.

7. **Standing doctrine.** (1) Content-addressed identity. (2) Phases = memo boundaries. (3) Every freeze carries unLawfulness budget. (4) Memoize under horizon; lazy outside; pre-memo toward attention. (5) Publish memos not just seeds. (6) Record every exo intervention. (7) Fork from last memo after law change; never discard mourning-hosted memos. (8) Moratorium fence in instantiation path at perception-grade.

8. **BREAK-1′ (flag).** Incremental phase-transitions (some systems carry, some sunset) may not form a clean DAG — entangled carries need explicit dependency tracking. Under-explored.
