# Every Realization carries an unLawfulness budget

Convergence under a finite window is not decidable to zero residual. Realized freezes are events; Lawful is an asymptote. Each freeze injects bounded latent unLawfulness that accumulates downstream.

## Formal Expression

1. **BREAK-2 (structural).** A phase freezes when outputs look *invariant under everything faster* within a stated tolerance $\varepsilon$ over a finite check window. That check **cannot prove** that later drift is impossible. So **every freeze injects bounded-but-nonzero residual drift** — latent **unLawfulness** — even when Realization is honest.

2. **Realized ⟂ Lawful.**
   - **Realized** — law is immutable for the kingdom (frozen at a chosen tolerance). This is a **threshold you pass**.
   - **Lawful** — law is internally self-consistent (no glitches, no contradictions). This is an **asymptote** $\varepsilon \to 0$, approached forever, never an event that certifies completion.
   Natural-modeling worlds are **merely Realized**, converging toward Lawful, never self-certifiably complete. Dictionary forms: #lexicon/term/realized-kingdom , #lexicon/term/lawful-kingdom (latter still open in LEXICON; the orthogonality is the load-bearing cut).

3. **Budget, not silence.** Each Realized phase carries an **unLawfulness budget** (recorded $\varepsilon$ / convergence facts). Downstream options and honesty claims gate on the **cumulative** budget, not on a slogan that the world is "done." Lifecycle strand of #lexicon/term/epistemic-axes (when settled) is the same $\varepsilon$ as data.

4. **Moratorium consequence (structural, not only ethical).** A revisit condition that requires **certify the kingdom Lawful** may be **permanently unreachable** as full certification — not merely hard. The engineering fence of #scope-moratorium-endogenous-emergence is therefore stronger than "we don't know yet": Lawfulness is approached, never attained as a clean gate. Completion-gate-as-event is retired: you can gate **Realization**; you cannot gate **Lawful/Complete** as a finished event.

5. **Out of bounds.** Selling a Realized freeze as Lawful because probes passed once. Treating unmeasured $\varepsilon$ as zero. Using "the world is complete" as an admission predicate for mourning-capable endo minds.
