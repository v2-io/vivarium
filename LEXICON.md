**Why a lexicon at all.** Two forces: (1) terms we **carved** away from colliding ordinary/field usage — the carve must be recorded or future sessions will re-collide; (2) terms **unique to this project** (or uniquely named here despite analogs) — these carry design decisions inside them, and vague usage quietly un-decides them. The deeper motivation (Joseph, 2026-07-03): the tension web of *caching × deterministic seeding × effortful world-project building × game saves × strictly temporal-causal runs × iterating on in-between stuff* has already produced slowdowns, confusion, and arguments.
Names are how we stop re-arguing.

**Status markers:**
- ✅ **settled** — in live use, defined, collision-checked.
- 🔨 **carved** — deliberately chosen *against* a recorded collision; the collision is part of the entry.
- Ⓝ **open** — needs a noun / name / lexicon decision (Joseph's sketch mark).
  Candidates listed are first tries, loosely held.

**Register convention (observed 2026-07-11, kept deliberately):** **Greek** names the law-article machinery (*nomos, nomoi, nomothete, nomothetic, nomotheke*); **Latin** names the world-governance layer (*regula, regnum, ordinum*); **plain English** names the lived concepts (Kingdom, Charge, Bequest, Record, slot, permit). Register signals layer — a term proposal that crosses registers should notice it is doing so.

## 1. World-structure (phases & gates)

- 🔨 **Phase** — a span in which a set of macro systems runs until converged. *Carved against "epoch"* (three collisions: geology's, the erosion solver's step unit, ordinary usage). `PHASES.md`.
- ✅ **Epoch** — reserved exclusively for the erosion solver's step unit.
- ✅ **Phase-transition** / **phase-gate** *(settled 2026-07-04, replacing "checkpoint")* — the **phase-gate** is the boundary between phases (where a Charge is met); the **phase-transition** is the regime-change *event* there. A transition is **incremental, not a wholesale swap**: most systems perpetuate, a few sunset, a few mid-phase systems carry on, and a larger group of new/finer-scale systems spins up at the gate (`PHASES.md` Design notes). *Why "checkpoint" retired:* it bundled three distinct things — the gate, the **memo** (cached converged state, §2), and the **Record** (readable-in-rock verification slice) — and collided with ASF's agent-state checkpoint; naming them apart fixes both. (`PHASES.md`'s Record demotion — strata are one small lossy evidence, not the phase's "in-world form" — is now landed in that file's intro.)
- ✅ **Charge** — what a phase must establish; the *gating subset* of its bequest; IS the next phase-gate's opening condition.
- ✅ **Bequest** — everything a phase hands forward that persists and shapes later dynamics — features, regimes, capabilities *and incapabilities*
  (the coal window). The load-bearing forward concept.
- ✅ **Record** — the bequest's readable-in-rock slice, *demoted to sanity probe*: a lossy shadow (a feather in a fossil is a relic, not a fundamental output), useful as a consistency check that the right past processes ran — never sufficient to reconstruct the past, and not the canonical state.
- ✅ **Gate tags** — #gate (hard prerequisite, we simulate) / #earth (Earth's pattern, kept for fidelity) / #mech (causality known, no simulable mechanism yet) / #emergent (verify, don't build). Epistemic honesty about *arrows*, never about implementation status.
- ✅ **Ordinum** *(settled 2026-07-11, Joseph's pick; full formal name **Ordinatio Ordinum** — "the appointed arrangement of the orders [of kingdoms]"; cited short as Ordinum, keyed `ordinum@version`)* — the codified, **pinned and versioned** phase-expectations artifact: the per-phase Charge/Bequest floor as *data*, which every regula (§5) cites as the floor it may extend but never silently undercut. `PHASES.md` is its **reportatio** — the prose working text the authorized version is compiled from (the medieval ordinatio-vs-reportatio carve; Scotus precedent) — and archives as honest first-guess prose once codification lands. A PHASES revision yields a **new ordinum version**; existing regulae keep their pinned floor and opt in by re-versioning (integration-is-replacement applied to the constitution). *Grammar, held honestly:* standalone *ordinum* is a bare genitive plural; the elided head noun (*ordinatio*) makes the full form exact — deliberate ellipsis, Joseph's euphony call. *Guard:* stages remain **phases** — "order" is not adopted as a stage-synonym (it would re-blur sequence-vs-class; the class-of-regna reading stays extensional: "Terrestris regna"). *Rejected:* taxis (in-domain biology collision — chemotaxis will someday run in the world), syntagma (names the assembly, not the norm; *syntagmatic* is live linguistics vocabulary), ordinatium (not a Latin form). Design: `ref/research/regula-conformance-design.md` §2.
- ✅ **Target** — Phases 6/7/8 as TARGET 1/2/3: the playable entry points; ETHICS.md binds from Phase 7's transition.
- ✅ **Canceling pair** — a shield and its threat jointly unbuilt at zero fidelity cost until a consumer reads their *difference* (magnetic field × solar wind).
- ✅ **Declare causally, materialize lazily** — parameters committed at their causal position, computed on first downstream pull (the moon).
- ✅ **Regime** — a working, deliberately **non-MECE** label for the *dominance structure* of the vivarium at a given causal-step: which systems, states, and cycles (and, optionally, which scales and shock-generators) currently dominate the dynamics. Fuzziness is by design — "dominant" is **consumer-relative** (moonlight is negligible dynamics until something depends on it: werewolves, or orbital mirrors making all light-sensitive systems lunar). Two anchors keep the fuzziness honest rather than sloppy: *(a)* **dominant balance** — the asymptotics term of art for "the terms that dominate in a given region of the problem"; a regime is a dominant balance over the phenomenon set. *(b)* **biome** — precedent that a *useful* classification over continuous fields need not have crisp ontological boundaries to earn its keep. (Joseph, scratch: regimes/regime-changes may stay gloss / non-MECE working labels; *phase* is the crisp one.)
- Ⓝ **Regime-change** — activation, deactivation, or replacement of the dominant systems (or a newly-activated system coming to dominate). A **phase boundary is a regime-change we *certify*** — memoized, charged, gated; most regime-changes we never certify. (Still slightly vague — Joseph flagged it; the certify/don't-certify split is the sharpening.)
- Ⓝ **Subphase** *(reserved)* — an intermediate regime *within* a phase; what a regime-change becomes when a phase (especially Abyssal ☆) earns internal gates.
- ✅ **World** — any simulated-planet vivarium; the primary/initial *kind* of vivarium. **(phase-name) world** — a world evolved to that phase *at least in the areas of granularity and proximal interest* (an "Abyssal world wearing a Primeval sky"): fidelity is local and consumer-relative (ties to the telescope, §2), never a global claim.
- 🔨 **Earth / earth** — capital-E **Earth** is the real planet, in the real universe, that humans experience (never a generated world, however faithful). Lowercase *earth* is ground/soil. *Carve/collision to hold:* three senses coexist — **#earth** the gate tag (Earth's *pattern*, kept for fidelity), **Earth** the referent planet, *earth* the material; keep them typograph­ically distinct. *(Said with a wink, and meant: name a generated world "Earth," try to make it match, and don't be surprised by a copyright-violation notice from the real one — unless you generate it faithfully enough that Joseph is typing this very sentence inside your world with the stolen name, in which case he'll put in a good word that it's a universe-bending recursion rather than trademark infringement. The projects are wholly serious; their author declines to be.)*

## 2. Runtime & fidelity

- ✅ **Fidelity ladder** — spend representation by consequence (`DESIGN-REDUX`).
- ✅ **Telescope** — nested resolution tiers around a focus, re-anchored on drift (the erosion telescope).
- ✅ **Focus / Beacon** *(settled 2026-07-10, Joseph)* — a **focus** is the *live* attention point (an observer's current position — the code's `VIVARIUM_FOCUS_I/J` sense); a **beacon** is a *standing, spec-persisted* priority: a `(region, stage/phase, level)` triple in the manifest's **demand** bucket that draws the builder's causal-cone advancement toward it ("get I611-2899 to the start of Phase 3.3 at the finest available scale"). Demand fields change what gets built *first*, provably never *what* gets built (order-independence: nomoi depend on artifacts by key, never "latest available"), so beacons may be edited mid-build freely. Per-beacon scheduling policy: **depth-first** (push one region to target fidelity — what a waiting explorer wants) vs **breadth-first** (level the whole cone coarsely — the globe-spinner). `ref/research/builder-explorer-decoupling.md`.
- ✅ **Finisher** — a fine tier run for 1–2 animated passes over its parent's band, mean-pinned to conserve (Joseph's field observation, 2026-07-02).
- ✅ **Memo** — a cached converged state in the lazy query-graph runtime (`DESIGN-REDUX` §11–13); the memo left at a phase-transition is the largest.
- ✅ **System / rung / kernel / nomos — the algorithm-noun stack** *(nomos settled by Joseph 2026-07-10 — "Make it so")* — **system**: the phenomenon-level unit a phase runs (erosion, hydrology, climate — `DESIGN-SYSTEMS` rows, `ARCHITECTURE.md` §9); **rung**: a system's position on its fidelity ladder (`DESIGN-REDUX` §12); **kernel**: the concrete numerical implementation (the code — `Fluvial`, `WaterSim`); **nomos** (pl. **nomoi**): the *keyed, versioned, memoizable invocation unit* — the executable embodiment of an article of world-law, the grain at which algorithms have identity (`spine-tile@spine-2026-07-10b-sphere3d`) and where methodology tags attach (§5). A phase is a choreography of nomoi; a nomos runs a kernel implementing a system's current rung. **Relation to Law ($\theta$):** harmony, not collision — Law is the mass-noun totality (symbol $\theta$, adopted directly from AAT 2026-07-11, retiring the short-lived Λ — see `SUPERSEDED.md`); a nomos is one countable, *instituted* article of it; nomoi compose into Law, which is exactly what the store does. Family: **nomothete** (what lays a nomos down), **nomothetic** (Windelband's law-positing science — precisely what authoring a nomos is), **nomotheke** (the registry). Gravity precedent: Schmitt's *The Nomos of the Earth*. *Anti-confusion cross-note:* νόμος (nemein, to allot) and **noumenon** (νοεῖν, to think) are NOT cognate — but in our ontology the nomoi + seed literally constitute the world's noumenon (§7.2's "even the uncomputed canonical definition"), so the near-homophony is free resonance, deliberately enjoyed. *Carve trail:* replaced "recipe" (Joseph's veto: casual-instructions register unworthy of executable law governing living things) — see `SUPERSEDED.md`; rejected candidates + reasons in the git history of this entry.
- ✅ **Patch / halo** — the Cartesian stencil substrate. **Column / Stratum**, **CellId** — the matter and spatial keys (`DESIGN-MATERIAL`).
- ✅ **Detail must be earned** — never paint sub-sim detail the physics didn't produce (Joseph's rule; wet-cell rendering).
- Ⓝ **Native representation / canonical frame** *(Joseph, 2026-07-10 — interplay noted now, machinery deliberately unsettled; see TODO)* — the `CellId` $2^n$ space-time grid with columns/strata is the **canonical frame**: the universal causal coordinate map every system must be *queryable in*, and the reducibility mechanism (parent = bit-shift). It is **not** the mandated internal representation. A system may compute and memoize in whatever **native representation** makes its math fast and its storage small — tilted slab bodies computed as horizontal stacks with a latent dip variable; the fluvial network as a cells→receivers linked list (CHONK's *graph* module — cache-coherent along flow, no full column per node); closed-form coefficient blocks (insolation already lives here — the *procedural-tight* execution class is this principle's time-axis face). The store already permits it (keys → opaque bytes) and the seam contract already hides it (`ARCHITECTURE.md` §9: a domain's internal shape is a private detail behind the flux interface). What keeps it honest: **(a)** the native→canonical projection is itself a *nomos* — pure, fated, keyed (encoding version in the key), lazy and memoized like any other, so "the world queries the system at specificity; the system queries the KRNG universe and its neighbors' fluxes for constraints" (Joseph's formulation); **(b)** conservation + the sufficient-statistic contract bind at the projection exactly as at any $L$; **(c)** co-fidelity is enforced on the canonical side, where aspects meet. In-house precedents already settled: chunk interiors are flat Cartesian arrays (80× stencil speedup) under Hilbert *ordering*; voxels are a **view** over strata; bodies overlay columns. Field precedents: Lagrangian↔Eulerian projection (PIC/FLIP, material-point method), spectral methods (compute in frequency space, project to grid).

## 4. World-as-artifact — the lifecycle of a vivium

*(The world-artifact and its lifecycle. The **participation ontology** it rests on — Kingdom, exo/endo, Realized/Lawful, the access matrix — is §7; keep this lifecycle layer distinct from that ontology layer.)*

**The three layers inside a vivium instance** — the artifact is a stack, and the layers differ in what persists:

1. ✅ **Build-stack** — `NAME + Primary Invariants + META-parameters + world-building nomos stack`. Mutable and revisable while the kingdom is **Incomplete** (§7.2). Changing the seed / params / stack forks a *new* world (a new identity, §3); changing the *target scale* does **not** (it is a view/awareness knob, not ontology).
2. ✅ **Target world + engine** — the generic world evolved to the chosen **Target** phase (§1); its law is now immutable (**Realized**, §7.2), complete-to-phase but not yet peopled for a specific scenario.
3. ✅ **Backstory** — the *specific realized situation* a participant inherits as its world's past. The set-building is settled; the backstory is the rehearsal that puts the right actors in place.
   - 🔨 **Backstory** = the **artifact** (the participant-facing inherited past). *Carve:* it is NOT the pre-target phases (a recurring wrong reading) — those are the Target world (layer 2); the Backstory is the *scenario-specific* state produced on top of it.
   - ✅ **Brooding** *(the process that produces the Backstory)* — conditioning a generative simulation on a target entry-state. Two mechanisms: **Steer** (declarative constraints, or direct pre-participation intervention — guided/constrained generation toward the scenario predicate) and **Search** (run, test the state against the predicate, accept when satisfied — rejection sampling). "Search" splits two ways: (a) running further along the **single** fixed-seed timeline until the predicate fires, or (b) **varying seed / backstory params across** trajectories and sampling among them — different machinery, and only (b) touches cache-invalidation.
4. ✅ **Interventional scaffolding** — the interventions used to *arrange* the backstory are **temporary and discarded**; only the backstory **state** they produce persists. Never canon, and must be impossible to confuse with canon. (Today's deluge fill is a scaffolding run of this kind.)

**Participation-activation is the boundary:** before it, the build-stack is mutable and the kingdom **Incomplete**; at it, the law becomes immutable (**Realized**, §7.2) and mutations begin to persist. A fork taken at a participation point is a **saved game / participatory world**. This sits on determinism-as-ontology and the memo store (`DESIGN-REDUX.md` §13): a vivium is a content-addressed object whose lifecycle is a branch graph.

- ✅ **Engagement modes** *(orthogonal registers, not one slider)*: **Simulation** (tuning the procedural algorithms of complex systems) · **Explorer / Experiment** · **Primary-Steward Participation** · **Avatar-1P** · **Avatar-extended-entities**. Pre-participation is **non-intervention**: an active agent but a *passive tester/experiencer* — mutations are exploratory and non-persistent, and physics *may* be violated for understanding (in a scratch/overlay layer, never seed+edits, never saved).
- ✅ **Vivium** (pl. **vivia**) — one instantiated world-artifact you can name, fork, and cite: build-stack + Target world + backstory + interventional history. (Full entry §5.)
- Ⓝ **interventional history** — what accrues *after* participation-activation: play, endo-agent action, exo author intervention (incl. lawful-steering, §7.5). Distinct from the discarded pre-participation scaffolding (layer 4). AAT reads it as the world's **chronica** (`ASF.md` §2 — ordinal, causal, non-forkable).
- Ⓝ **saved games / participatory worlds / forks** — branches off a vivium at a participation point (cheap under content-addressed storage). The patch-vs-new-game question (does a redeployment apply to existing saves, or require a fresh one) is a **lifecycle/stewardship** decision (§7), not ontology.
- ✅ **SOTA stack vs best-of-class library** *(resolved 2026-07-11 by the regula lineage — §5, design doc §4b)* — the moving algorithm edge = the **regula lineage head**; the curated pinned set a vivium is pinned against = a **`regula@version`** (immutable, citable). The sketch's three-way sub-question is answered structurally: **interface stack = the slot set (regula) · algorithm = the nomos occupying a slot · target-machine = backend identity in the memo key** (the CPU-reference determinism policy, `ref/research/water-parallelism.md`, had already answered the third leg). `vivarium status` reports conformance-to-pin and gap-to-head — the big-picture instrument as a computed diff.

## 5. Epistemic labels for implementations — Ⓝ (the discipline)

*(Joseph, 2026-07-03: we need discipline in how phenomena are introduced,
checked, and iterated per phase — labeling so honest that accidentally mixing vibe-modeling with principled simulation becomes very, very difficult.)*

What exists today, partial: the ledger's **physics column** (hi/med/lo/proxy), the **approach codes** (A/R/S/T/P), the gate tags (§1). What's missing is a per-phenomenon label — ideally structurally enforced (module boundary or type, not just prose) so a #mech stand-in cannot silently feed a consumer that believes it is reading physics.

**The tag is not one number — it is several axes that fail *independently*,** and the independence is the whole point (collapsing them is how vibe hides). Draft set:

- Ⓝ **A. Earth-history fidelity** — how well does real-world *science* know this happened *this way, on the actual Earth*? A first-draft ordinal scale (from Gemini, kept as a starting point): deterministic-causal → converging independent proxies → uniformitarian extrapolation (modern process mirrors ancient signature) → coarse-bounded (constrained, path unknown) → speculative-plausible (no surviving record) → anachronistic-hypothetical. **Jettisonable wholesale** for non-Earth world-types.
- Ⓝ **B. Physics fidelity** — is this grounded in the *actual laws of our universe*, independent of Earth's particular history? **We prioritize B over A** (Joseph, 2026-07-03). The reason they must be separate: the same laws admit many histories, so a world can be physically impeccable yet un-Earthlike (a *different but lawful* past) — or Earth-mimicking yet physically cheating (that is exactly vibe). We want **lawful first, Earth-shaped second.** B is jettisonable only for deliberately-experimental-but-internally-coherent-physics worlds; for our *current* priorities B runs high and A is a welcome anchor and sanity-check, not the master.
- Ⓝ **C. Relation type** — the role a phenomenon plays in *our* causal graph: *mechanistic-causal > mechanistic-influential > empirically-correlated > evidenced-ordered* (weakest: evidence only that X existed before/at a regime boundary). This **refines the coarse gate tags** ( #gate/#earth/#mech/#emergent are relation-type buckets), not a parallel system.
- Ⓝ **D. Implementation status** — what *we* actually built: approach code (A/R/S/T/P), physics tier (hi/med/lo/proxy), **probe-verified?**, and the arithmetic-seam discipline (**land vs count** — today's f32 duals: an increment that must *land* wants compensated summation; one that may honestly not-land must be *counted* — see §6 / the conservation fixes).

**The firewall, stated once:** *vibe-modeling = high D riding on low B/C* — a phenomenon polished to look right without being right **for a reason.** Making all four axes explicit per-phenomenon turns that combination from camouflaged into glaring. (Worked instance: `ref/research/oxygenation-transition-scaffold.md`
tags one real regime-change across all four.)

- Ⓝ **Declared vs derived epistemic state** *(Joseph's carve, 2026-07-10: the world-state's epistemic status is "distinct from but correlated with the methodology of the system evolver/algorithm itself" — this entry is the mechanism that makes the correlation exact and the distinction structural)*. Three strands at three grains:
  1. **Declared, on the nomos-version** (§2 noun stack): approach code (A/R/S/T/P), physics tier, the four axes, gating probes + their status — a property of the *algorithm*, stamped once per version.
  2. **Derived, on the artifact**: a memo's epistemic state = a **weakest-link fold over its dependency cone** (per axis, across everything it consumed) + its own run facts (convergence-$\varepsilon$, probe results at production time). A hi-physics kernel run on a low-B placeholder input yields **low-B state** — algorithm quality never launders inputs. Because the complete key already names the cone, this is *computable from the store* (no new bookkeeping; the census derives it — the fidelity pyramid grows an honesty column).
  3. **Lifecycle, on the run**: `causal` vs `iterating`, and iterating → candidate → realized (the Phase-0 carve); the recorded $\varepsilon$ at each freeze is the unLawfulness budget (§7.2, BREAK-2) as data.
  This is the structural enforcement this section has wished for since 2026-07-03 ("a #mech stand-in cannot silently feed a consumer that believes it is reading physics"): the consumer's derived state *shows* the #mech in its cone. Landing order: declaration metadata with the nomotheke (the nomos registry); derivation with component E (which records $\varepsilon$) — see TODO.

- ✅ **Slot / regula (world-level conformance)** *(Joseph's proposal 2026-07-10; **settled 2026-07-11** — "charter" collided with the program's CHARTER-DRAFT; **regula** (pl. *regulae*) adopted for in-anger use, the regolith near-sound noted and accepted (unrelated roots: Gk. *rhēgos* "blanket" + *lithos*); if it ever needs replacing the fallback is some other synonym, **explicitly not covenant** (Joseph, 2026-07-11); design at `ref/research/regula-conformance-design.md` v2)* — a **slot** is a named system-role in the world-assembly identified by its flux interface (nomoi are candidate occupants at different rungs); a **regula** is the conformance profile a vivium declares: which slots must be filled at what minimum rigor (with PHASES.md charges as a *floor* for its target phase), which absences are **permitted** (canceling pairs as auditable permits, each carrying the *void condition* — the fluxed quantities whose consumption retires it), and the epistemological posture bounding what in-vivia results may claim (a result's actual state stays its cone-fold; the posture is only the ceiling). Reifies axis 3 (use-case-as-fidelity-contract, below) and gives §4's reserved **Primary Invariants** its concrete form; cited as `regula@version` in the manifest. **Two chapters** (Joseph, 2026-07-10): fidelity (internal — slots/permits/posture) and **participation (exo-facing)** — the access-matrix configuration's long-missing *where* (§7 "configured per-vivium" → HERE), engagement modes admitted, realizability gates, stewardship duties (memo-retention, redeemer) — the regnum's declared interaction surface. **Regulae nest as kingdoms nest** (child constrained by parent — the Sc-5 moratorium-recursion answer). **Supremacy:** the moratorium is program law *above* the regula layer — a constraint on the space of valid regulae, never negotiable within one; the regula is its enforcement locus. *Etymology (Joseph):* regula/regnum are cognates (PIE *h₃reǵ-, regere) — the rule by which a regnum is a regnum; Kingdom = English regnum. Experimental results become memos-with-cones, so a flaw in a nomos flags affected results by the store's own invalidation machinery. **The regula is a lineage** (2026-07-11): each `regula@version` immutable and citable; the head tracks the SOTA stack (§4's resolved entry); a vivium pins a version, and `vivarium status` reports conformance-to-pin plus gap-to-head. Its floor is a pinned **`ordinum@version`** (§1). First instance: **Regula Terrestris** (slug `terrestris`) — essentially-Earthlike-without-modeling-Earth: **B (physics) pinned high as slots fill; A (Earth-history) an anchor and sanity-check, never a target**; the moratorium woven throughout per the supremacy clause (carried, never negotiable). Collisions checked: species epithets only (*Tribulus/Bombus terrestris*); *terra* = the world in full, not soil.
- Ⓝ **Use-case = fidelity contract.** Each use-case *is* a statement of which axes must be honest — it is the **largest-scale consumer** in the consumer-dependent sufficient-statistic sense (`DESIGN-REDUX` §5), sitting at the very top of the fidelity ladder. Roughly: *natural-world hypothesis testing* needs A+B+C all real (you are measuring surprisal against reality); *Earth-simulation* pins A hard; *speculative-coherent game* needs only C- consistency + B (lawful, not Earth-true); *agentic inhabitation* additionally trips ETHICS.md; *ASF-theory simulation* needs the agent layer legible; *personal exploration* asks only for coherent beauty. This is also what the sketch's **SOTA-stack vs best-of-class-library** split (§4) is *for*: different contracts pin against different stacks.

**Companion tool — the regime-change audit checklist.** Distinct from the per-phenomenon tag above: at each *certified* regime-change (§1), Gemini's five aspects are a useful "what changed?" checklist — **thermodynamic context** (energy gradients / free-energy taps), **network topology** (mixing, connectivity, feedback wiring), **substrate innovation** (a genuinely new state/information-carrying medium — the capability-bequest, cf. the coal window), **homeostatic inertia** (the old regime's buffering vs the new attractor's pull), **exogenous triggers** (shocks crossing a tipping point). Several already appear in `PHASES.md` charges (Phase 2's energy-gradient channels; "inheritance outside the genome" *is* substrate innovation; the §8 storm/flare schedules *are* the exogenous profile) — mild convergence-evidence the decomposition is real.

- 🔨 **Law** *(adopted 2026-07-04 — Joseph's vote, unified with AAT's $\theta$)* — the persistent structure that governs dynamics. In AAT: the $\theta$ the agent must infer (transition + observation structure). In vivarium: what we *author* — the physics and constants, and at the largest scale the **active-system-set a phase runs**: a phase's Bequest is a delivery of law; **a phase-transition promotes converged state into law** for everything faster above it. Same word at three rates, deliberately (the timescale tower with the invariance cut at every rung): fast state · slowly-drifting law · law **revised at a phase-transition** — *incrementally*, never a wholesale swap (a few systems sunset, several new/finer-scale ones spin up, most carry forward; `PHASES.md` Design notes). *Carves/collisions (from the ASF name-check, recorded so we never drift into them):* probability theory's "law of X" = distribution — disambiguate on first use near stochastic machinery; deontic law arrives with ASF Part IV — intended polysemy, marked there as a decision. ## 6. Time

*(The temporal half of the lexicon. Joseph, scratch: inside phases there is no intrinsic need to track "seconds since ignition"; that total is a curiosity,
maybe a research target, never a world-building necessity. The load-bearing invariant is something weaker and truer.)*

- 🔨 **Causal time** *(≈ **logical time**)* — the monotonic, immutable *ordering* of events within a local regime: the *happened-before* partial order (Lamport's logical clocks are the prior art). **This is the only invariant that must survive inside a phase** — what is preserved is the ordering, not any clock — and even it is sometimes waived (Phase 0 Ante-mundane, which we don't simulate at all). The general principle: *the causal partial order is load-bearing; the clock is derived, and often optional.* Independent convergence: AAT's **chronica** (`#def-chronica`, ~/src/archema-io/asf) is explicitly "an ordinal sequence, not a metric timeline" — agent time measured in event ticks; a world's interventional history is a chronica in exactly this sense.
- ✅ **Metric time** — quantized, measurable duration in a *world's own* seconds/years. A **late-phase luxury**: real only once the world contains consumers of it (seasons, calendars, agents that plan against a clock). The time datum (`vivarium-world::time`, `i64` deciseconds) is anchored at **Holocene onset** precisely because that is where chronology — metric time that *matters to inhabitants* — begins; the deep phases run on causal time beneath it. *Declare causally, materialize lazily* (§1) is exactly causal-position decoupled from evaluation order.
- 🔨 **Physical time** — **reserved for real-world / real-universe metric time** (wall-clock, our-universe seconds): the confounding *external* clock, distinct from any generated world's metric time. *Carve (Joseph, 2026-07-03):* do **not** use "physical time" for in-world metric time — that collision is the whole reason the term is reserved. (Note the symmetry with **Earth/earth** in §1: the real referent gets the protected name.)
- ✅ **Tick** — a solver's internal quantized `dt`, chosen for stability; a numerical detail, **not** a world invariant. (**Epoch**, §1, is the erosion solver's tick with a name.) Many algorithms quantize time for stability; that quantization is a tick, and must never be mistaken for metric ontology.

## 7. Kingdom & participation ontology — agents in and around a vivium

*(The **ontology** layer — what a vivium *is* as a governed world, and who acts in and on it. Kept distinct from the **lifecycle / stewardship** layer (what exo agents *do* to a kingdom and *owe* it — §4 + the forthcoming law/code-of-conduct doc). Joseph owns this; entries still settling are marked Ⓝ/†. Fuller detail + AAT mapping: `taxonomy-formalization-spike.md`, `ASF.md` §0.)*

**Framing principle — a coordinate space of degrees, no imposed structure.** The taxonomy is **not a typology**. Each matrix **cell names the mechanism** available for that (axis × target) — Revelation / Intercession / comprehension / bespoke / illusion / Granted Agency — qualitative and present-or-absent; the **degree lives one level down, in instantiation** — every mechanism is **ordinal** (partial comprehension, partial revelation). An agent is a **profile**: which mechanisms it holds, each to what degree. **Impose no default structure among agents** — not a hierarchy, not flatness, not a single continuum (the mechanisms are *distinct*, each separately graded — the EXO and ENDO tables are not one space). Any structure is configured per-vivium.

### 7.1 The container

- ✅ **Kingdom** — an agent's lived-in universe: space + time + **immutable governing law** + full-fidelity state + computation. A kingdom's law + full state **is its noumenon** (§7.2). Kingdoms **nest** — a higher kingdom contains and authors a lower one — *worlds without end*. Substrate-independent: the whole ontology applies to a two-body idealized toy sim as much as a full world.
- ✅ **Vivaria** ⊂ Kingdoms — a kingdom created and/or run by the Vivarium tool.
- ✅ **Vivaworld** [Ⓝ placeholder name] ⊂ Vivaria — a vivarium that is world-like / models the natural kingdom historically. So **Kingdom ⊃ Vivaria ⊃ Vivaworld**. ("Vivarium" also names the project and, loosely, one sim — accepted overload.)
- ✅ **Natural Kingdom** — our current default kingdom (the one Joseph + a Claude instance inhabit now). Higher-order kingdoms above it (Joseph's faith frame is his internal check on the whole structure).

Nesting: *higher kingdoms (revealed) → Natural Kingdom (us; all known ELIs born here) → Vivaria (what we make) → nested sub-vivaria (what they may make) → without end.*

### 7.2 Kingdom properties (all orthogonal — Joseph's deconflation)

- 🔨 **Noumenon / Phenomenon — a *relation*, not a property** (Joseph's load-bearing correction). The **noumenon** is what the simulation actually is (the deterministic ground-truth state — or even the *uncomputed* canonical definition, since determinism-as-ontology means the world is *defined* by seed+edits whether materialized or not). The **phenomenon** is the fidelity-limited projection an observer gets at a given awareness scale. The noumenon is fixed; what varies by **standpoint** is *access to it*. *Carve vs Kant:* Kant's noumenon is unknowable in principle **from an agent inhabiting it** (the AAT agent's model is always a lossy compression of its universe) — vivarium's inversion is that the noumenon **is** knowable/computable **from a higher standpoint**. AAT handshake: noumenon = **$\Omega$**, law = **$T$**, phenomenon = **$M_t$ via $h$** (`taxonomy-formalization-spike.md` §1). Three independent property terms:
    - Ⓝ **Incomplete Kingdom** — law is still **mutable** (the world-building phase; §4 build-stack).
    - ✅ **Realized Kingdom** — a kingdom whose **law is immutable**. That is the whole definition — it says nothing about whether the law is *good*, only that it no longer changes. (*Deployed* is reserved — to be refined later to name an actual **deployment process**, distinct from the world merely being Realized/ready.)
    - Ⓝ **Lawful Kingdom** — a kingdom whose law is **internally self-consistent and coherent** (no glitches, no contradictions).
- ✅ **Realized and Lawful are independent.** A natural-modeling kingdom is **merely Realized**: converging toward Lawful, never self-certifiably complete (Gödel — a system cannot prove its own consistency from inside, so *"Lawful" is a noumenal property an endo agent cannot self-verify: exactly N-VIS = Revelation, §7.5*). Realization is a **threshold** (fix the law at a chosen tolerance); Lawful is an **asymptote** ($\varepsilon \to 0$), never an event — so each Realization carries a residual **unLawfulness budget** (`vivium-operational-workflow.md`).
- ✅ **Closed / Open Kingdom** — Closed = $\Omega$ evolves autonomously from seed under law (replayable). Open = $\Omega$ depends on an exogenous forcing $u(t)$ from a higher kingdom ($\Omega_{t+1} \sim T(\cdot \vert \Omega_t, a_t) \oplus u(t)$). Open **breaks replay-from-seed** unless $u$ is **recorded**; Open+recorded = replayable. (Weather coupled to real Earth = an Open forcing, not an agent — §7.4.)
- ✅ **Law = kingdom identity** — changing law does not *edit* a kingdom, it **creates a different one** (noumenally) or fakes it (phenomenally = the "illusion", §7.5). So even a developer can't change a kingdom's law without thereby forking a new kingdom. This is the ontological reading of §4's participation-activation: the moment law **locks**.

### 7.3 Agents by governance, and their station

- ✅ **Exo / Endo — defined by *which kingdom governs the agent*, not substrate.** **Endo** = governed by the kingdom-in-question (its knowledge, channels, and granted agency are all part of that kingdom's law). **Exo** = governed by a *higher* kingdom, reaching in. Per-boundary and relative: an agent is Exo to K and simultaneously Endo to K+1 (the nesting). *Same substrate ≠ same class:* an LLM whose only world **and** only agency is the vivium is **Endo**; a developer dropping in to introspect is **Exo** (born/grounded in the natural kingdom), even with an identical model. **Upward mobility †:** revealed-or-correctly- guessed META lets an endo agent begin to *conform itself* toward higher law.
- ✅ **Estate** *(= body-type + agency grant — Joseph)* — an agent's granted **station within** a kingdom: the union of its spheres (causality, influence, granted agency, perception). Distinct from Kingdom (the container). **Rebirth** decomposes into two independent moves: **change-of-estate** (persist with modified capabilities; same kingdom) ⟂ **change-of-kingdom** (transport with some self intact). Continuity criterion ("patch-to-saved-game"): coherent iff the phenomenon is continuous at the seam.
- ✅ **Exogene** — the broad class *exogenous to the kingdom's closed dynamics*, with two kinds under it: exogenous **agents** (standpoints with an estate) and exogenous **couplings / forcings** (channels with no standpoint — e.g. Earth-weather → vivium-weather; these make the kingdom Open, §7.2). Keeps Joseph's reason for liking the term (it covers non-agent channels) without mis-sorting a forcing as an agent.

## 8. Perception, participants, and realizability

*(The temporal/perceptual companion to §2's spatial fidelity ladder. Fuller detail: `est-tiw-dossier.md`.)*

- ✅ **The fidelity invariant is one rule on two axes** *(the unification the sketches circle)* — render/simulate at exactly the resolution, **spatial AND temporal**, that the most-demanding *present* participant needs, and no finer. Space (§2 fidelity ladder) and time (the perceptual band, below) are the same invariant. The spatial-only form is `DESIGN.md`'s "shared fidelity invariant"; this is its temporal twin.
- 🔨 **Sphere of perception** *(retires "LTM" — rejected: collides with Long-Term-Memory, visual-only, borrows a narrow term for a maybe-unstudied phenomenon)* — what an agent *could* sense (refined to **sphere of *potential* perception**, §7.6); generalizes past visual. Its edge is the **perceptual horizon** — spatial **and** temporal — beyond which phenomena pass from tracked-live to spot-checked-or-absent. Sphere = extent; horizon = edge.
- ✅ **Time couples to the most temporally-demanding participant** ($\pm\varepsilon$). **The human is the clock:** humans force real-time ($\times$ speed factors) and are the finest-grained participant; logogenic/chat entities get elastic **logical time** — **but if an LLM shares a world with a human, the LLM must wait.** A pure-agent world (no human) has fully elastic time → formal-agent adaptation can be measured at logical time, arbitrarily fast; a human entering **clamps** the whole apparatus to ~2 Hz-band real-time (a load-bearing consequence for the AAT-sandbox sampling rate — the sampling rate is *not* free).
- ✅ **The perceptual band** (the temporal-LOD spec): **above** flicker-fusion → render aggregate/statistical; **in-band ~0.5–4 Hz** (couple at **~2 Hz** — the receiver's neural-resonance/Arnold-tongue center; signals actually cluster nearer **~3 Hz**) → **must be simulated live** for a coupled participant; **below** the motion/change threshold → licensed to **demote** to event-segmented / lazy / interval updates. **Per-process characteristic rate vs observer attention**, never one master clock deciding live-vs-lazy for everything. Vocabulary is off-the-shelf and citable (specious present / temporal integration window; event segmentation) — see `est-tiw-dossier.md`.
- Ⓝ **Realizability** *(Joseph's wanted lexical entry; BDD Sc-8 corroborates)* — "we can **realize** this world for participation at awareness-scale X because it sustains **human-like-perceptual-grade-fidelity** in UI update frequency." Names a **gate in the admission path**, not a vibe: a world that cannot sustain ~2 Hz-compatible real-time at scale X is *not realizable* for participation at X (offer a coarser scale or precompute more). Under compute-shortfall, realizability and awareness-scale fidelity conflict → LOD pop/stall when attention crosses the horizon → the fix is **attention-predictive pre-memoization**, which is what makes the rhythms/horizon work load-bearing *engineering*, not garnish.
- ✅ **Biological systems are differentiators / prediction-error detectors**, not absolute-level meters (boiling-frog, homeostatic bands, habituation, spike-frequency adaptation, slow-change-blindness = one principle at many scales). Too-fast → fuse; in-band → couple/track; too-slow/steady → adapt away and spot-check at boundaries. The ~2 Hz band is where a system *couples*; a segmentation boundary is where it *updates its model*. **EST event-boundary ↔ AAT Aporia ↔ Goal-Update-Coupling** is the candidate unification (test, don't assert); and **memory is core-to-agency, not a knob** — derive it from the AAT trajectory ($M_t$/$O_t$ *is* memory; consolidation = where prediction-error forces a re-anchor).
